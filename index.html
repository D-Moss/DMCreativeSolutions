About Me

Hi, I’m Diona, the creator behind DM Creative Solutions.

Before starting DMCS, I was working multiple jobs. During COVID, everything slowed down, and that’s when I found my way into tech. I started with a course in Information Technology and Customer Service, and after completing it, I wanted to learn more. What started as curiosity quickly turned into something more—it wasn’t something I planned. I fell into it, and it turned out to be one of the best things that ever happened to me.

I found HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and from there, I was hooked. Creating websites became more than just something to learn—it became a creative outlet. It feels like painting. I start with a blank canvas and watch my ideas come to life on the screen. As a self-taught developer with multiple certifications, I’ve continued to build my skills, creating multiple sites and growing my confidence along the way.

At the same time, writing reviews has always been something I’ve enjoyed. I rely on reviews to make my own decisions, so I wanted to provide that same value to others. I’ve written reviews on platforms like Google and Amazon, always focusing on being honest and helpful. As an Amazon Vine reviewer and someone whose Google reviews reach thousands of people, I’ve seen firsthand how much reviews can influence and help others.

DM Creative Solutions came from combining both of these passions—creating websites and writing honest reviews. I wanted to build something of my own, something that reflects what I enjoy doing while also helping others. Since this is something I’m truly passionate about, I’m willing to work hard to create something that reflects someone else’s passion just as much as my own.

Whether it’s designing a website or sharing a review, my goal is to create work that people can trust.

DMCS continues to grow, and my vision is simple: to build a brand that provides value, attracts a strong following, and connects with people who appreciate honest content and thoughtful design.
